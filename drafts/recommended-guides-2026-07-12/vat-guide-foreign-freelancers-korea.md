### Why VAT is easy to mix up

Freelancers often focus on income tax or 3.3% withholding. VAT is a separate tax area that can matter when services, invoices, and business registration are involved.

### What triggers a closer review

Regular clients, tax invoice requests, business registration, and large recurring payments are signals to ask for advice.

### Keep clean records

Separate invoices, receipts, expenses, and payment confirmations. Clean records make both VAT and income tax review easier.
