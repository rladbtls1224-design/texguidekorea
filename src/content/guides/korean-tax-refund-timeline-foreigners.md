### Why refund timing varies

Refund timing depends on how the refund arises. An employer year-end settlement refund may be handled through payroll, while a filing-related refund may be processed through official tax systems. Freelance 3.3% refund reviews may follow a different workflow from simple salary adjustment.

Avoid comparing your timeline too closely with a coworker's or friend's timeline. Their income type, filing date, employer process, and bank account situation may differ.

### What should you check?

Check whether the filing was submitted, whether the refund bank account is correct, and whether any notice asks for more information. Use Hometax or Sontax if available.

| Status issue | Possible check |
| --- | --- |
| No payment yet | Review official status and bank account |
| Amount changed | Compare filing summary and notices |
| No record found | Confirm tax year and filing route |
