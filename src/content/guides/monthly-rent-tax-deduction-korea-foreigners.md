### Why rent deductions are not automatic

Rent feels like a normal living expense, but tax treatment depends on eligibility. The fact that you pay rent in Korea does not automatically mean it reduces your tax.

### What to prepare

Keep the rental contract, bank transfer records, landlord details, and address records. Ask payroll early because employers often have document deadlines for year-end settlement.

### When to get help

Ask for help if your contract is not in your name, payment proof is missing, or your housing situation changed during the year.
