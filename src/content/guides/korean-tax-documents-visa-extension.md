### Why visa tax documents are stressful

Visa extension deadlines can make tax documents feel urgent. The challenge is that document names may be similar, and the office may ask for a specific year or issue date. A certificate that is useful for banking may not be the same as the one requested for immigration.

Start by getting the exact document list. If possible, ask for the Korean names of documents.

### Common document types

The most common tax-related documents include certificate of income, tax payment certificate, and withholding receipt. Employment certificates or bank records may support the application but may not replace official tax certificates.
