### Verify before paying

A real tax notice should be checked through official channels. Be careful with payment links, messages, or requests that do not match your records.

### Read the key fields

Find the tax type, tax year, deadline, amount, and office contact. These fields decide the next step.

### Keep proof

Save the notice and any payment confirmation. If you call a tax office, write down the date, contact point, and answer.
