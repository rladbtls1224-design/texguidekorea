### Before you leave a job

Ask payroll what tax documents you should keep. A withholding receipt and final payslip can save time later when your new employer or a tax professional reviews your annual records.

### During year-end settlement

Tell your current employer that you changed jobs during the year. Ask whether previous salary and tax withheld were included. If not, check whether separate filing review is needed.

### After settlement

Compare the result with Hometax and your own payslips. If something is missing, contact the employer or payer before relying on a refund estimate or income certificate.
