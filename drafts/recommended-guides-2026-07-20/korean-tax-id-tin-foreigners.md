### Start with the requester and form

Ask whether the requester needs a personal Korean tax identifier, a business registration number, or a foreign-country TIN. Obtain the form name and instructions instead of sending every number you have.

### Match the identifier hierarchy

NTS English form instructions commonly prioritize a Korean resident or business registration number. For a foreign individual without that number, the form may use a domestic residence report number, foreigner registration number, passport number, investment registration number, or foreign-country TIN in a specified order.

### Match the official name

Use the English name, order, spacing, and date of birth shown on the relevant official record. A TIN can be valid while a portal rejects it because the name or registration data does not match.

### Protect the number

Treat registration and taxpayer numbers as sensitive personal data. Use official encrypted portals or secure upload tools, and mask all but the necessary digits in routine correspondence.
