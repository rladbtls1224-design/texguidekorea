### What Hometax is used for

Hometax is the main online tax portal in Korea. Foreigners may use it for income tax filing, certificate issuance, tax payment, refund status checks, and income record review. It is important because many tax and document tasks eventually connect to official records.

The hardest part is often access. If your name, phone, registration details, or authentication method does not match, login can fail.
