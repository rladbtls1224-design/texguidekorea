### What if the certificate is blank or incomplete?

A certificate usually reflects income reported through official channels. If expected income is missing, the cause may be timing, payer reporting, income category, or a wrong period selection. Ask the payer for a withholding receipt or correction path, and confirm with the tax office if needed.

For visa extension, do not assume the office will accept a different document. Requirements can vary by visa type and office practice, so verify the list before submitting.
