### What should you check after payment?

After payment, return to Hometax or the official service to confirm the status where possible. Keep proof of payment with the tax year and amount visible. If a visa office or bank later requests a tax payment certificate, your payment may need time to appear in official records.

If the payment relates to a filing you did not understand, ask for help before making additional payments or corrections. Tax payment mistakes can be harder to fix under deadline pressure.
