### Why registration questions come up

Freelance income can start casually, then become regular. When clients ask for invoices, tax invoices, or business details, business registration questions become more important.

### Check tax and visa separately

Even if tax registration is possible, your visa may limit work activity. Check both before changing how you invoice or receive income.

### Get advice before switching

Registration can affect filing, VAT, expenses, and documents. Ask a tax office or qualified professional before making the change.
