### Creator income is usually mixed income

Creators may receive ad revenue, sponsorships, affiliates, donations, course sales, consulting fees, or platform payouts. Each stream should be listed separately.

### Cross-border issues matter

Foreign platforms and overseas bank accounts do not automatically answer Korean tax questions. Residency, work location, and treaty issues may matter.

### Get help early

If creator income becomes regular, ask a professional before filing season. It is easier to set records correctly from the start.
