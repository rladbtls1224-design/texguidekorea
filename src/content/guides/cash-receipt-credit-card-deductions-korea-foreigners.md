### Why spending records matter

Korean year-end settlement can include data from card spending and cash receipts. For foreigners, the practical problem is often identity matching and understanding what is eligible.

### Check identity details

If cash receipt records are tied to the wrong phone number, old registration details, or another person, they may not appear as expected. Check early.

### Ask payroll

Employers often give a submission guide. Follow that guide and keep copies of any records you submit.
