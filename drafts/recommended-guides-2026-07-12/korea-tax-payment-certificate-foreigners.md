### Why this certificate causes confusion

Foreigners are often asked for "tax proof" without a clear English document name. That request may mean a tax payment certificate, a certificate of income, a withholding receipt, or another official record. Before issuing anything, ask for the Korean document name and the year or period.

### What to check before issuing it

Confirm whether the office wants national tax, local tax, or a general tax status record. If the request is connected to a visa or bank process, also check whether the document must be recently issued.

### When to ask for help

Ask Hometax support, Government24 support, a local tax office, or a qualified tax professional if the certificate shows unexpected unpaid tax, missing records, or a different name than requested.
